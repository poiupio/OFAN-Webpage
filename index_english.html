Product

OFAN is a system that supports making productive decisions based on your impact on the profitability of agribusiness exploitation.

It allows the registration of all operational data of biological and productive processes, in addition to direct and indirect costs of production, administration, sales and marketing, etc. to generate operational performance indicators and economic through a cost process based on biological data and a mathematical model of agroindustrial production.

Generates productive, commercial and economic reports for each company, area, farm, lot and shed, by week, month or year, for which it is integrated easily with other production, accounting and administrative applications.

It allows the analysis of different cases and future scenarios, using a productive mathematical model and the application of forecasting methods specialized allowing the elaboration of a better operating budget based on to the profitability results and not only in the productive ones. Facilitates the evaluation of current results by contrasting against the scenarios or budgets previously generated. OFAN generates the necessary information to support the management and company management enriching business processes such as obtaining the cost of production, performance evaluation and its indicators, forecast of consumption, costs and sales, budgeting and monitoring actual result. OFAN provides optimization tools to determine the parameters productive (age, weight and consumption) that generate the highest profitability in the production process and allows analyzing the behavior and impact of the same before variations in prices and costs of markets, products, inputs, expenses and factors of production.

OFAN allows management to follow up on the real business data and compare it with projected data, to detect areas of opportunity or update circumstances.

Through a Microsoft Excel visualization interface that interprets and analyzes the data presented, in the repository of the information, to focus time and resources on decisions.

Thanks to the scoreboard in color code and graphics, allows identifying not only the defined indicators, but also how and because the variables of said indicators changed.
